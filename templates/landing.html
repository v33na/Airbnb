Summary: Latitude and Climate

Our analysis objective to review how climate variables can change based on degrees of latitude. We reviewed over 500 cities from the OpenWeatherMap API for the results.

Once the data was munged, we analyzed the data with a variety of plots for cloud cover, temparature, wind speed and humidity. Please see the results of the findings on this site. It will include visuals (MatplotLib), data (Pandas) and explanations of our observations of the data. Some of the results are predictable such as warmer temperatures closer to the equator, but others show variations such as wind speed.
